⚡ How to Use This Guide
1
Duplicate this Notion page — click "Duplicate" at the top right so you get your own editable copy
2
Read the budget breakdown first — decide if the trip fits your current budget before planning dates
3
Book your bus 1 week in advance — HRTC buses fill up fast on weekends, especially May–September
4
Screenshot the cab price table — you'll need it offline since there's no network beyond Kasol
5
Download Maps.me offline before leaving Delhi — works without internet on the trek
Note: Prices in this guide are based on 2025 student trips. Bus fares and hostel rates may vary slightly by season. Always check HRTC website directly for current bus prices before booking.
